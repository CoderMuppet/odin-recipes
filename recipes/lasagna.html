Kim's Lasagna

After years of tweaking this lasagna recipe, I've finally got it to where I want it. Everyone raves about it just the way it is, although I'll probably never stop tweaking it!

This is great if you prepare it the day ahead and keep it refrigerated. Just add 10 to 15 minutes to the cook time if baked cold from the refrigerator.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the sausage and ground beef until browned and cooked through.
  3. Drain excess fat and return to the skillet.
  4. Add onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  8. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese and egg.
  9. Layer half of the noodles in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Spread half of the meat sauce over the noodles.
  11. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the meat sauce.
  12. Repeat layers in same order.
  13. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until golden brown on top.
